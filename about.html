About

Tyana is a third-year design student and a hopeful future UX/UI designer. What brought her to UX/UI design was first taking computer science at York University. After realizing that it was not for her, she branched into more user-specific programs and that is when she knew she really enjoyed UX/UI design.

Tyana is currently working on my Honour's Bachelor degree for Digital Experience Design at George Brown College. She has gained new skills and built on old ones. George Brown has really taught her what she needed to know and showed her how much more there was.

She aims to pursue a career in UX/UI design while specializing in front-end development. Tyana continues to build her skillset in hopes of gaining an internship in the future to further her career.
