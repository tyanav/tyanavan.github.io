Branding

Summary

The purpose of this project was to create branding for a local startup business. Logos, wireframing and social media content were created for this brand.

Timeline

November 2020 - December 2020

Research

Local competitors were researched to see what the market was like and who the target audience was. Large chain companies were also discusessed for social media content as well as involved in the creation of branding and wireframing. Here is a quick brainstorm of what the coffee shop can be. Different topics and words were bounced back and forth and everything was written down to make sure that nothing would be forgotten. There are no bad ideas!

Tools Used

Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe XD

Deliverables

The final product was an app mockup of what the coffee shop could do in terms of an app and other additional features that they wanted to include. An instagram layout board was created to determine the aesthetic and how they wanted to show themselves as a company.

Instagram Layout 1 - The first is an overall view and aesthetic of the entire page. The next three mockups are what content posts would look like.

Instagram Layout 2 - Additional Instagram content pages and what they could look like.

Mockup app pages of potential app interface. Includes startup, login, sign-in and home pages.

Mockup app pages of potential app interface. Includes menu and item description, mobile ordering, receipt and order review pages.
